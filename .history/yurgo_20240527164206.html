About Yurgo

Yurgo is the perfect app for challenging people and bringing exciting competition to the world. This platform gives everyone the chance at becoming a winner, in ANY niche. Users can interact with your friends with comments and fan likes. Yurgo give users an opportunity to be acknowledged, boost their status, and connect and compete with people across the world! What’s better than a little friendly competition? Everyone gets better and it’s incredibly fun putting your creativity to the test!

My Role & Development Details

I was frontend develepor who also worked with the CEO to create high fidelity mockups of UI designs. Mockups were designed with AdobeXD and the app was coded using Swift 5 UI Kit. Videos were edited in Adobe After Effects and Final Cut Pro X. See below for a video featuring some of my work—comparing my high-fidelity designs to their coded versions.
